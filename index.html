Welcome to my GitHub page

I am researcher at the BCAM-Basque Center for Applied Mathematics an research leader of the Applied Statistics Group.

I obtained my PhD. in Statistics at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, previously I was Postdoctoral Researcher at CSIRO Mathematics, Informatics and Statistics division (old CMIS) now Digital Productivity Flagship in Melbourne VIC Australia (from February 2011 to February 2014).

Research interests

My research interest lies in non-parametric smoothing methods for complex data. Particularly I'm studying smoothing techniques based on penalized splines regression models (Penalized Likelihood splines with B-splines basis) in mixed models framework ("Generalized Linear Mixed Models", GLMM's). My advisor was Dr. María Durbán. I'm also member of the Statistical Modelling Society since July 2006 and since 2016 I am member of the board of the Spanish Biometric Society The main applications of my work are related to:

  • Multidimensional smoothing
  • Spatial and spatio-temporal statistics
  • Computational Statistics
  • Disease mapping and epidemiology
  • Mortality Life Tables
  • Forecasting with P-splines
  • Environmental modelling
  • Functional Data Analysis
  • Wireless Sensors and Sensor Networks
  • Health Related Quality of Life
  • Complex data visualization
